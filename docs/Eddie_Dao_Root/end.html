The End

After the battle, the village celebrated their freedom, finally liberated from the Empire. Yami continued to grieve for his brother and pondered his future as the new emperor and how he wished to rule, but he knew something needed to change. The day after the celebration, Yami said his goodbyes to everyone in the village and led his brother’s soldiers back to the Empire.

It had been a long time since Yami had been home, and he carried with him new ideas for his Empire. He named it Hikari Yami—Light and Darkness—an Empire that would rule fairly and peacefully, guiding and protecting all people.
