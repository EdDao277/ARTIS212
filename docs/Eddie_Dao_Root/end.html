The End

After the battle, the village celebrated their freedom finaly over the Empire. Yami continue to grief over his brother and ponder his future as the new emperor and how he wish to rule, but he knows something needs to change. The day after the party Yami said his good byes to everyone in the village and led the soldiers that came with his brother back to the empire. As it has been a long time since Yami been home and has some ideas of his new empire. The empire will be called Hikari Yami, Light and Darkness, that will rule fairly and peacefully above everyone and to help protect everyone.
