Blossoming of a New Empire

The ruling family of the Empire suffered a huge toll: the emperor and empress were assassinated, and one of the princes was kidnapped by the Shadow Clan, leaving only Hikari to rule the growing Empire. The deaths of the emperor and empress plunged the Empire into turmoil. More wars broke out, leaving the realm Hikari’s father built in ruins. Driven by hatred toward the Shadow Clan, Hikari vowed to rebuild what his father had created for him and his brother.

This pushed Hikari to finish his studies and master the art of the sword so he could become an emperor like his father. Once Hikari became the rightful ruler, he set out to avenge his family. He would have to reunite the Empire and take back the lands lost to rebellions. After regaining control over his Empire, nothing seemed to stand in his way.

His desire for revenge made him an ambitious ruler. He laid claim to the rest of the land in the hope of destroying the Shadow Clan. Yet there was no sign of them; it was as if they had never existed. Although Hikari took over the whole country in his search, he never found an army that fought like them. Frustrated, Hikari placed a bounty on the Shadow Clan—anyone who could find their land would be rewarded. Now Hikari waits in his throne room, hoping they will be found.
